Update Report Status and Close Report

1. Click on reports

2. Click on the report you'd like to update

3. Click "Details"

4. Click the status field

5. Click the status you'd like to assign from the drop down

6. Optional: Click the "Please provide a comment or description." field.

7. Type in your comments on the status update.

8. Click "Save new status"

How to view past status updates

9. Click "History"

10. Here you can view past report status updates, and the status in purple.

How to cancel a status update

11. Once you have clicked the status, click cancel

How to close a report

12. Navigate to the Details page of a report

13. Click "Close report"

How to re-open a report

14. Click "Reopen report"
